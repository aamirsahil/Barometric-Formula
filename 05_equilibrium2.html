Substituting the values, $$ (P_{i+1} \times A + \rho Agdz) - P_i \times A = 0 $$

Rearranging, $$ (P_{i+1} \times A - P_i \times A) = - \rho Agdz $$ $$ (P_{i+1} - P_i) = - \rho gdz $$

Which is, $$ dP = \rho gdz $$
